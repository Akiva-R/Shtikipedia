## Proposal

Rashi holds that מוכת עץ gets a כתובה of 100 because it is a פגם, but by אדם it is not a פגם because it is normal. therefore, even though a גדול would make her a בעולה גמורה, a קטן would have her remain a בתולה גמורה.
## Source
1. Rebi Meir and the חכמים argue if a מוכת עץ gets a כתובה of 100 or 200.
2. Rashi on יבמות נט says that there is an opinion who holds that a כה"ג is מותר to כה"ג.
3. Maybe we can say that the מחלוקת of whether or not she is מותר to כה"ג is the same as whether or not she gets כתובה of 100 or 200.
4. We can't say that, because the גמרא say's רבי מאיר compares to בוגרת, which he holds is אסור to כה"ג.
5. Rashi holds that the מחלוקת of רב and שמואל is by היכר בתולים.  Rav is easy to understand, but why does shemual say gets full 200, she should at least be מוכת עץ?
6. Rashi must hold that problem of מוכת עץ is פגם, but by אדם is not פגם because it is normal. However, unless it is a קטן, the ביאת אדם makes her a בעולה גמורה.
