## Proposal

The רמב"ם does not bring the דין of כל שלא משומש, because it only applies in יהודה
## Source
1. Rav ashi says that in יהודה, one is not believed to claim פתח פתוח without שימוש.
2. The רמב"ם leaves out this הלכה. Seemingly, it is because it is irrelevent outside of יהודה.
3. Q: The אחרונים ask, The רמב"ם (אישות יא יב) says that one can claim פ"פ even if their is דם. if so, why does רב אשי need שימוש. How is no שימוש worse than דם לפנינו?
	1. The בית שמואל answers that having no שימוש is worse because seem like רמאי.
	2. The הפלאה answers that case of רב אשי is where he checked, even though no שושבינן, so if he checked but no דם we think it is him. case of רמב"ם is where he didn't check before so we think it could be fake דם.
