## Proposal

The גרא brings a proof from the fact that the חכמים lowered the כתובה of an אלמנת כהן that even a כתובה of an אלמנה which is for sure דרבנן is כסף צורי.
## Source

1. The גמרא says that originally the כתובה of a בתולה כהנת was 400 and 200 for אלמנה.
2. Then they had trouble getting married because people would say I might as well marry a בתולה ישראלית for the same price, so חז"ל lowerred it.
3. The גרא brings a proof from here that a כתובה of an אלמנה must also be כסף צורי (assuming that one of a בתולה is), because otherwise it is not the same amount.
