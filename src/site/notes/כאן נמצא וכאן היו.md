### Facts

1. The משנה on יב:ב says that ר"ג belives the woman that משארסתני נאנסתי because of ברי together with a מיגו, or ברי with a חזקה. In the משנה on יג we belive her that she is כשר לכהונה.
	+ In the firsts case, there is a שינוי רשות, but in the 2nd there is not.
2. The גמרא on טז:א says that רבן גמליאל works through ברי.
	+ It is unclear if this agrees with the משנה on יב and the גמארא on עה.
3. The משנה on עה:ב says if the husband finds מומין, if it is בבית אביה, the father needs to prove it came after ארוסין. If בבית הבעל, the husband needs to prove they were there from before.
	1. The גמרא asks that acc ר"ג the בעל should always need to bring proof, and for רבי יהושע, the woman should always need to bring proof?
		1. Ruvu answers the difference is כאן נמצו וכאן היו.
		2. Rav Ashi answers the difference is weather the woman is claiming her own money or her father's moneys.
			1. Rashi explains this that the father can't use his daughter חזקת הגוף. 
				+ The ר"ן asks why would this be.
			2. The ר"י explains that only she can use her טענת ברי, but then conludes that it works with the חזקת הגוף by itself. 
				+ It is hard to understand how either of them would learn the גמרא on טז.
			3. The תוס הרא"ש explains like the ר"י that it is based on ברי.
				+ Based on this he can say that the גמרא on טז is going like רב אשי.
			


## If רבא says that a woman needs a כאן נמצו to be belived, then why on יג is she belived when she is מעוברת?


1. The תוס הרא"ש says that רבה doesn't need a כאן נמצו in the סיפה for the woman to be able to collect. 
	1. This can't mean that we don't need it at all because if not the חזקה would be קטיעא. Rather he means that the חזקה of the woman is enough for her to collect, and the only problem would be that it is קטיעא, but if it was found in the בעל's רשות then it is not קטיעא.
	2. He is not bothered by מעוברת, because that is just a רעיותא, not קטיעא, and he holds that a חזקה with a רעיותא would work, even for ממון.
	3. He can explain the משנה on יב:ב as either רבא and it's like the סיפה since there is no רעיותא, so it does not even need the ברי, or like רב אשי, that it works together with the ברי.
2. Rashi on the other hand learns that on עה there is just a רעיותא, so although she has a חזקה, it is not strong enough to be מוציא ממון.
	+ He must explain that the משנה on יב is where there was time for ביאה after נישואין, so her חזקה does not have a רעיותא בבית האב. This would then be just like the סיפה on עה, and would seem to work even withou a ברי, just with a חזקת הגוף.
3. The תוס ר"ש on the other hand learns that we need the כאן נמצו in the סיפה. 
	1. The שב שמעתא explained this that כאן נמצו means we have no ספק in the בעל's רשות, so she does not even need a חזקה to be belived.
	2. He must then say that כאן נמצו is only nescassary for ממון, which is why on יג a מעוברת is מותר to כהן just with a חזקת הגוף.
	3. He explains that on יב there is time after נישואן, so it is like רשות בעל, and yet still explains we need ברי and חזקת הגוף. Why?
		1. It must be that he holds that if the חזקה is קטיעא, then even ברי + חזקת הגוף is not enough, to be מוציא ממון. 
		2. If so, then shouldn't it now not be a ספק, why do we need חזקה + ברי?
			+ It must be according to ר'אשי.
4. The ר"ן explains that the מחלוקת רבא and רב אשי is only b/c she is a שמא, but on יב then work even ברשות האב.
	1. Seem like כאן נמצו works even w/o a חזקה. Must be that it tells us there is not even a ספק.
	2. He can explain טז that no רששויות so need חזקת הגוף + ברי.
