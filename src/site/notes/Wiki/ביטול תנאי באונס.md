## Proposal

The ר"ן holds that one can not מבטל a תנאי באונס, but the רא"ה holds you can
## Source

1. The רא"ה on כתובות דך ב says that if one is מקדש a woman on condition he **does** come at a certain time, and an אונס stops him from coming it is not a good קידושין, even though it was an אונס, because in the end of the day he did not come, so the תנאי was not נתקיים. He says that the same would be true by גירושין.
2. The ר"ן on קידושין סא argues and says although by קידושין it is not a good קידושין because we need her דעת(and she only agreed to get married if he comes - שך חו"מ כא), by גירושין it is still a good get because she came באונס.
3. Q: How does the ר"ן make sense, if in the end of the day he was not מקיים the תנאי, how can it be a good גירושין?
4. A: The גרנ"ט answers based on his יסוד that there are 2 types of תנאים, see [[Wiki/תנאי קיום ותנאי ביטול\|תנאי קיום ותנאי ביטול]]. This is clearly a תנאי ביטול, of if I don't come it should not be קידשין, so unless he actively does not come, it is a קידושין.
5. Q: Then why does the רא"ה say it is not a גט?
6. A: The רא"ה did not say that if he does not come באונס it is a problem of קיום התנאי באונס, but rather that it is like the מעשה גירושין is באונס. (Rav Shimon exoplains that when one make a תנאי conditional on his דעת, he is really saying that he want to retain free will, and if is אונס, it is not free choice). This however only applies to making a חלות, that you can't do it באונס (see [[Wiki/ולנערה לא תעשה דבר\|ולנערה לא תעשה דבר]]). However, there is no rule that you can't **not** divorce someone באונס.
