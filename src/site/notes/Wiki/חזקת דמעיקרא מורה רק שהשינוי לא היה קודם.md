## Proposal

The אבני נזר says that a חזקה דמעיקרא can only tell us that a שינוי did not happen before the time we are מסופק about, and not that it did occur afterwards.
## Source

1. If a baby is born ספק בה"ש and ספק בשבת, we have to push the ברית to Sunday, because if he was born Friday, then שבת is not זמנו, and only a ברית בזמנו is דוחה שבת.
2. Q: The אבני נזר asks the mother has a חזקת מעוברת, so we should assume the baby was born on שבת?
3. A: Our question isn't if born on ערב שבת, but if born on שבת (because the fact that he was not born on ערב שבת does not cause the ברית on שבת), and on שבת she for sure was not pregnant, so there is no חזקת הגוף.
