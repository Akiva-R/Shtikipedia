## Proposal

The רא"ש says that the reason people are מיקל on חדש is because ס"ס. We explained this that the היתר of being from last year, and the היתר of the עומר are 2 seperate התירים.
## Source

1. The רא"ש (שו"ת ב:א) brought by the רמ"א (יו"ד רצג) says that we are מיקל about חדש because of  a [[Wiki/ספק ספיקא\|ספק ספיקא]]:
	1. Maybe it was from last year's grain
	2. Even if it was from this years grain, maybe it was השריש before פתח.
2. There are multiple problems with this:
	1. It is שם אחד (מהרי"ט אלגאזי & רעק"א).
	2. It is not מתהפך (see [[Wiki/ספק ספיקא שאינו מתהפך\|ספק ספיקא שאינו מתהפך]]).
3. The גמרא on קידושין לז:ב darshens the  פסוק of _ויאכלו מעבור הארץ ממחרת הפסח_(יהושע ה:יא) that they were trying to get around the איסור חדש.
	1. The רעב"א asks, but ממחרת הפתח does not mean טז, it means after שחיטת הפסת on טו?
	2. Rabeinu tam answers that they ate from the previous years crop through טו, and then they ate the new years crop on טז.
		1. Q: How does it help that is was the previous year's grain, they did not bring the עומר in the מדבר, so it should still be חדש.
		2. A: It seems that only grain from this year needs the היתר of עומר, but grain from last year is מותר even w/o the עומר.
4. This would solve both problems with the רא"ש:
	1. It is not שם אחד, it is 2 different היתרים.
	2. We don't have the problem of [[Wiki/ספק ספיקא שאינו מתהפך\|ספק ספיקא שאינו מתהפך]], because as the שך explained, that doesn't apply if it only makes sense to look at it one way, and here it only makes sense to start by asking if it is from this year and the איסור חדש even applies, and if it does then we can ask if it was השריש before or after the עומר.
