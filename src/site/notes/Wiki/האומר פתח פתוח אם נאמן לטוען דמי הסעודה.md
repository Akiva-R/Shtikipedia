## Proposal

The בה"ג says that if one claims פ"פ he is נאמן to collect the value of the סעודה from his wife.
## Source

1. The תוס הרי"ד (י:א) quotes the בה"ג that if the husband claims פ"פ he is נאמן to make her pay him the value of the סעודה.
	+ The רי"ד argues.
2. The בה"ג seems to hold that פ"פ is נאמן as a ודאי, not just that he's פטור from paying the כתובה because of a ספק. 
## Challenges

1. The גמרא explains that the only reason he is נאמן to be מפסיד is because כתובה is דרבנן and הם האמינוהו, if so how is he נאמן to collect the דמי סעודה? Also, through what is he collecting it, she is at most a מזיק בגרמא?
	+ The רמב"ן on ג:א quotes the רי"מיגש that we can force the husband to be טורח for the סעודה (see [[Wiki/גדר תקנת שקדו\|גדר תקנת שקדו]]). It would seem from here that paying for the סעודה is part of the חיובי בעל to his wife, so if it was בטעות then הם האמינוהו to collect it back.
